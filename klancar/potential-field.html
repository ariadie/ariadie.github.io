📐 Parameter & Persamaan

\(k_{\text{attr}}\) — konstanta gaya tarik. Semakin besar, robot makin cepat tertarik ke goal.

\[U_{\text{attr}}(q) = \frac12 k_{\text{attr}}\,d^2(q, q_{\text{goal}})\]

\[-\nabla U_{\text{attr}} = k_{\text{attr}}\,(q_{\text{goal}} - q)\]

\(k_{\text{rep}}\) — konstanta gaya tolak. Semakin besar, robot makin kuat menjauhi obstacle.

\[U_{\text{rep}}(q) = \begin{cases} \frac12 k_{\text{rep}}\!\left(\frac{1}{D} - \frac{1}{D_0}\right)^2 & D \le D_0 \\ 0 & D > D_0 \end{cases}\]

\[-\nabla U_{\text{rep}} = k_{\text{rep}}\!\left(\frac{1}{D} - \frac{1}{D_0}\right)\!\frac{1}{D^3}\,(q - q_{\text{obst}})\]

\(D_0\) — radius threshold tolakan. Di luar radius ini, obstacle tidak memberi gaya.
