Kumpulan catatan interaktif yang mengubah bab-bab inti dari buku Russell & Norvig, Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach, menjadi teori yang mudah dibaca beserta simulasi JavaScript langsung. Setiap bab mencakup definisi, rumus kunci, latihan terselesaikan, dan simulasi interaktif. Gunakan kartu di bawah untuk melompat ke suatu bab.

1

Introduction to AI

Definisi AI, empat dimensi pendekatan, kondisi rasionalitas, sejarah AI (Dartmouth hingga Deep Learning), tipe agen, dan framework PEAS. Dilengkapi simulasi PEAS Builder interaktif.

Baca Bab 1 → 2a

Intelligent Agents: Types & Environments

Tipe agen (human, robotic, software), agent function vs agent program, rasionalitas, sifat-sifat lingkungan (6 properti), dan contoh PEAS. Simulasi interaktif sifat lingkungan.

Baca Bab 2a → 2b

Agent Structures

Simple Reflex, Model-Based, Goal-Based, Utility-Based, dan Learning Agent. Simulasi diagram blok arsitektur agen interaktif.

Baca Bab 2b → 3a

Problem Formulation & Uninformed Search

Formulasi masalah (state space, aksi, transisi, goal test, biaya), BFS, DFS, Uniform-Cost Search, dan Iterative Deepening. Dilengkapi simulasi pencarian pada graf.

Baca Bab 3a → 3b

Informed Search: A*, Heuristics & Local Search

Greedy Best-First, A* Search, fungsi heuristik (admissible, consistent), Hill Climbing, Simulated Annealing, dan Genetic Algorithm. Simulasi A* pada grid 2D.

Baca Bab 3b → 4a

Beyond Classical Search: Nondeterministic & Contingency

Lingkungan nondeterministic, AND-OR search, contingency plans. Simulasi interaktif pohon AND-OR.

Baca Bab 4a → 4b

Partially Observable & Online Search

Belief state, sensorless planning, online search agents, LRTA*. Simulasi eksplorasi grid online.

Baca Bab 4b → 5

Adversarial Search & Games

Minimax, Alpha-Beta Pruning, tipe-tipe game. Simulasi Tic-Tac-Toe vs AI.

Baca Bab 5 → 6

Constraint Satisfaction Problems

CSP, backtracking, MRV, AC-3, forward checking. Simulasi pewarnaan peta Australia.

Baca Bab 6 → 7

Logical Agents: Propositional Logic

Knowledge-based agents, propositional logic, knowledge base, entailment, model checking. Simulasi truth table interaktif.

Baca Bab 7 → 8

First-Order Logic

Sintaks & semantik FOL, quantifiers, Wumpus World dalam FOL. Simulasi interpreter FOL interaktif.

Baca Bab 8 → 9

Inference in First-Order Logic

Unifikasi, forward chaining, backward chaining, resolution. Simulasi visual unifikasi step-by-step.
